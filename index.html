ABOUT US

Computer Science (CS) and Information Technology (IT) is a decisive factor in business and industry, but it is also a space for creativity and self-development for all ages and profiles. we believe the best way to obtain and exploit CS and IT is through embedding it in our daily life(home, school, work ...).

ELLabs (embedded learning laboratories), research and consulting office, aims to develop and enhance CS and IT fields by creating educational curricula and professional training courses, as well as focusing on developing the technics and methodologies for integrating it in educational institutions and companies operating in the sector.
